YOUNG ARCHITECTS BOOTCAMP 2023

The Architecture Bootcamp is a year-long intensive program designed to expand the knowledge of high school students in architecture, hone their skills in design and prepare them for their careers. Led by a team of seasoned professionals, the bootcamp covers a wide range of topics, from design principles and construction techniques to project management and sustainability.

Over the course of the program, participants will work on a variety of real-world projects, both individually and in teams. They will learn how to create detailed architectural plans, develop 3D models, and use cutting-edge software to bring their designs to life. In addition, they will gain hands-on experience with materials and construction methods, visiting construction sites and participating in workshops led by industry experts.
